The message

Space careers start with curiosity, practice, and clear next steps.

Students do not need to know everything before they begin. They can ask better questions, build simple prototypes, learn from mistakes, and grow into a path that once felt too far away.

Five-step plan

A simple path from interest to action.

01

Start with Wonder

Use launches, rovers, astronauts, and mission control moments to make the goal feel real.

02

Find a Role

Explore how pilots, engineers, coders, doctors, builders, writers, and designers all support missions.

03

Build Skills

Connect math, science, reading, coding, creativity, and teamwork to realistic mission tasks.

04

Practice Missions

Test ideas through hands-on challenges, badges, and small design choices.

05

Plan What Comes Next

Choose next steps for this week, this year, and later in high school or college.
